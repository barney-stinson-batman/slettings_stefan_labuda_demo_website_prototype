Our Services.

From a simple let-only service to comprehensive property management — we offer everything a landlord needs, tailored to your exact requirements.

01

Let Only

Ideal for landlords who want to manage their own property but need help finding the right tenant.

Our Let Only service covers everything needed to find you a quality tenant and get your tenancy off to the right start. We handle all the legwork so you can hand over a fully-referenced, legally-compliant tenancy ready to manage yourself.

This service is perfect for experienced landlords who are confident managing day-to-day property issues but want the reassurance of a professional agency finding and vetting their tenants.

What's Included:
✓ Professional property marketing
✓ Accompanied viewings
✓ Full tenant referencing & credit checks
✓ Right to Rent checks
✓ Tenancy agreement preparation
✓ Deposit collection & registration
✓ Move-in coordination
02

Rent Collection

All the benefits of professional tenant-finding plus hassle-free monthly rent collection.

Our Rent Collection service gives you everything in Let Only, plus we take on the responsibility of collecting your rent each month and chasing any arrears — removing the awkward conversations and administrative burden from your plate entirely.

Ideal for landlords who want consistent monthly income without the stress of dealing with rent payment issues directly.

What's Included:
✓ Everything in Let Only
✓ Monthly rent collection
✓ Rent arrears management
✓ Monthly landlord statements
✓ Funds transferred directly to you
Most Popular
03

Full Property Management

The complete, hands-off solution for landlords who want total peace of mind.

Our Full Management service is exactly what it says — we take care of absolutely everything on your behalf. From the initial property marketing and tenant search to day-to-day management, maintenance coordination, legal compliance and tenancy renewals.

You simply sit back and receive your rental income each month while we handle every aspect of your property. Whether you live locally or overseas, this service gives you complete confidence that your investment is in safe, professional hands.

What's Included:
✓ Everything in Rent Collection
✓ Full day-to-day property management
✓ Maintenance requests & coordination
✓ Contractor sourcing & supervision
✓ Regular property inspections
✓ Tenancy renewals & rent reviews
✓ Legal compliance management
✓ Gas safety, EPC & licensing
✓ End of tenancy & deposit disputes
✓ 24/7 emergency contact for tenants
