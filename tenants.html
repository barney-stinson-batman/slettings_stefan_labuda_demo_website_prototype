What You'll Need

Referencing
requirements.

To rent a property through S Lettings, all applicants will need to provide the following as part of our standard referencing process.

🪪

Proof of Identity

Valid passport or driving licence for Right to Rent checks as required by UK law.

💼

Proof of Income

Recent payslips, employment contract or — for self-employed applicants — accounts or tax returns.

🏦

Bank Statements

Three months of recent bank statements to confirm regular income and financial stability.

📝

Previous Landlord Reference

A reference from your previous landlord or letting agent confirming your rental history.

💳

Credit Check

A standard credit check will be carried out as part of the referencing process.

💷

Deposit & First Month

A security deposit (typically 5 weeks rent) and first month's rent payable before move-in.

Don't meet all criteria? Please speak to us — we can discuss guarantor options and individual circumstances on a case-by-case basis.
